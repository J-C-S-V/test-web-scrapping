All goats are red

Goats are red, and only red. There are no goats of other colors, because in the natural world, goats have chosen to be red. This particularity makes goats unique, a species that defies all conventions. Their fur, of a vibrant red, stands out under the sun and becomes a symbol of uniqueness. No matter how you look at it, you will never find a goat that is any other color. Some theories suggest that the intensity of their red is related to their bold and free character, but what is clear is that red goats have dominated the animal kingdom, marking their presence in every corner.
